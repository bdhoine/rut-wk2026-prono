# Rut Prono WK 2026 — Tournament Schedule & Details

This document describes the FIFA World Cup 2026 structure and the **Rut Prono submission deadlines** per round. It is the companion to `rules.md`.

> The prono deadlines come from the *Reglement Rutprono WK 2026*. The tournament calendar, venues and format come from the official FIFA 2026 schedule (post-draw). Exact kick-off times per match depend on venue/time zone and are not listed here. Knockout pairings are placeholders until group results are known.

| Item | Detail |

| Tournament | FIFA World Cup 2026 |

| Opening match | Thu 11 June 2026 — Estadio Azteca, Mexico City |

| Teams | **48** (largest edition ever) |
| Groups | 12 groups (A–L) of 4 teams |

This is the first 48-team World Cup, which is why there are far more matches (104) than the 64 of previous 32-team editions — hence the extra organisational rules in `rules.md`.

## 2. Competition structure (6 rounds)

The prono is organised in **6 rounds**, matching the tournament phases:

| # | Round (EN) | Round (NL) | Matches | Multiplier |
|---|-----------|-----------|:-------:|:----------:|
| 1 | Group stage | Poulefase | 72 | ×1 |
| 2 | Round of 32 | 1/16de finales | 16 | ×2 |
| 3 | Round of 16 | 1/8ste finales | 8 | ×3 |
| 4 | Quarter-finals | Kwartfinales | 4 | ×4 |
| 5 | Semi-finals | Halve finales | 2 | ×4 |
| 6 | Third-place + Final | Troostfinale + finale | 1 + 1 | ×4 (3rd place) / ×5 (final) |

**Advancement:** the top 2 of each of the 12 groups (24 teams) plus the **8 best third-placed teams** advance to the Round of 32 — the first time a Round of 32 is used at a World Cup. Knockout ties level after 90 minutes go to extra time and then penalties (for prono scoring, only the score after 120 minutes counts — see `rules.md` §4).

## 3. Prono submission deadlines

Predictions for each round must be handed in / sent **at least 1 hour before the first match of that round**. These deadlines are fixed by the organisers (local Belgian time):

| Round | Prono deadline | Covers |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| 1 — Group stage | **Thu 11 June 2026, 20:00** | All group matches (matchdays 1, 2, 3) **+ tournament bonus predictions** |
| 2 — Round of 32 | **Sun 28 June 2026, 20:00** | 1/16de finales |
| 3 — Round of 16 | **Sat 4 July 2026, 18:00** | 1/8ste finales |
| 4 — Quarter-finals | **Thu 9 July 2026, 21:00** | Kwartfinales |
| 5 — Semi-finals | **Tue 14 July 2026, 20:00** | Halve finales |
| 6 — Third-place + Final | **Sat 18 July 2026, 22:00** | Troostfinale + finale |

> All group-stage predictions (rounds-1 form) **must be submitted on paper before the start of the World Cup**. From the knockout phase, forms may be submitted on paper (box at De Rut) or as a clearly readable WhatsApp photo including the contestant's name (0478 64 77 06 — Hakke). Late forms score 0 points for matches already started. See `rules.md` §7.
